| T-18-06 | Elevation | nav item href injection into the `<a href>` sink | mitigate | nav propsSchema reuses `noAbsoluteScheme` (rejects scheme + `//`); the render also never binds an onClick/navigation handler (Phase-19 deferred), so the href is display-only.
