<objective>
Close the signal-flow gap (D-05) so the studio can render four honest generation states, and ship the
two pure helpers the studio frontend (Plans 15-02/15-03) consumes. This is the ONLY backend/contract
change in Phase 15 — purely additive, no new generation/cache/renderer logic.

Three additive layers thread `outcome` + `cacheHit` to the client:
1. Python use-case result (`GenerateUiSpecResult.outcome`) — return the already-computed
   `_determine_outcome(...)` value on the cold path; `"ok"` on the cache-hit short-circuit.
2. FastAPI view (`GenerateUiSpecView.outcome`) — additive field populated from the result.
3. tRPC `GenerateOutputSchema` — extend to `{ outcome, spec, cacheHit, reason? }`, reading
   `data.cache_hit` + `data.outcome` from the envelope while keeping the authoritative web-boundary
   `SpecRootSchema.safeParse` override in place.

Plus two pure, framework-free helpers in `@nauta/genui/studio`:
- `deriveGenerationState` — the D-04 four-state mapper.
- `describePropsSchema` — the D-11 Zod introspection for the catalog prop table.

Purpose: STDO-04 (four states distinguishable from real signals) + the testable foundations of STDO-01
(catalog prop table) — with automated tests, since `apps/web` has no test runner.
Output: extended contract on three layers + two unit-tested genui helpers.
</objective>
