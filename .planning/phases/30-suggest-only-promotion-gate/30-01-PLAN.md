Make synthesis emit human-reviewable *suggestions* (INFERRED/AMBIGUOUS) that nothing acts on, ship
the single sanctioned auto-injection read gate that returns only EXTRACTED edges, and surface tier
through the tRPC graph payload so suggestions are visibly distinguished from trusted truth (TIER-02;
ROADMAP SC1 + SC2).

are ever trusted for auto-injection" becomes literally true and test-enforced.
Output: extended synthesizer, two new entity-instance reads, a `list_injectable_edges` gate + seeded
three-tier exclusion test, and a tier/is_active-aware tRPC graph seam.
</objective>
